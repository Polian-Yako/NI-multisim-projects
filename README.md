# NI Multisim Projects Collection

## 📋 Repository Overview
This repository serves as a curated collection of advanced electronic circuit designs and SPICE simulations developed using **National Instruments (NI) Multisim**. The primary objective of these projects is to design, model, and rigorously validate the behavior of complex analog and digital electronic systems prior to physical prototyping.

## 🎯 Key Focus Areas
*   **Schematic Capture:** High-fidelity electronic schematic drafting utilizing industry-standard component libraries.
*   **SPICE Simulation:** In-depth transient, AC, and DC sweep analyses to evaluate circuit performance and signal integrity under various operational conditions.
*   **Analog & Digital Circuitry:** Comprehensive modeling of diverse electronic architectures, ranging from analog amplifiers and filters to digital logic gates and sequential circuits.
*   **System Validation:** Theoretical verification of electronic concepts and mathematical models through empirical simulation data.
